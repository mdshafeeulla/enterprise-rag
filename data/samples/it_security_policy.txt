# IT - Cyber Security and Password Policy

## Objective
To ensure that all employees use secure passwords and follow best practices to protect the company's digital assets.

## Password Requirements
1. **Length**: Minimum of 12 characters.
2. **Complexity**: Must include at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, one number, and one special character (e.g., !, @, #, $).
3. **Uniqueness**: Passwords must not contain the employee's name, username, or common words.
4. **Rotation**: Passwords must be changed every 90 days.

## Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
MFA is mandatory for all remote access and cloud-based services (e.g., Email, VPN, ERP).

## Prohibited Actions
- Sharing passwords with anyone, including IT support staff.
- Writing passwords on sticky notes or storing them in unencrypted files.
- Reusing passwords from personal accounts.

## Reporting
If you suspect your password has been compromised, notify the IT Security Desk immediately at security@company.com.
