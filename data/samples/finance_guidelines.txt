# Finance - Expense Reimbursement Guidelines

## Purpose
To outline the process for employees to claim reimbursement for business-related expenses.

## Eligible Expenses
1. **Travel**: Airfare (economy class), train tickets, and mileage for personal vehicles used for business.
2. **Accommodation**: Hotel stays within the company's approved budget (max $200/night).
3. **Meals**: Up to $60 per day for overnight travel.
4. **Office Supplies**: Purchases under $50 with prior manager approval.

## Submission Process
- Claims must be submitted via the Finance Portal within 30 days of the expense.
- Digital copies of receipts are required for all expenses exceeding $25.
- Manager approval is mandatory for all claims.

## Payment Timeline
Approved reimbursements are processed every Friday and paid via direct deposit.

## Non-Reimbursable Items
- Alcohol (unless part of a client dinner).
- Personal grooming or laundry services (for trips under 4 days).
- Traffic fines or parking tickets.
