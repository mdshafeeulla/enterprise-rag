# Legal - Data Privacy and NDA Policy

## Overview
Protection of sensitive company and client data is a legal requirement. All employees must sign a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) upon joining.

## Data Classification
1. **Public**: Information intended for general consumption.
2. **Internal**: Information for company use only (e.g., internal memos).
3. **Confidential**: Sensitive data (e.g., client lists, financial records).
4. **Restricted**: Highly sensitive data (e.g., trade secrets, M&A plans).

## NDA Obligations
- Employees must not disclose confidential information to any third party during or after their employment.
- All company-provided equipment must be returned upon termination of employment.

## Handling Data
- Encrypt all files containing Personal Identifiable Information (PII).
- Do not use personal cloud storage services (e.g., Dropbox, Google Drive) for company documents.

## Compliance
Violations of this policy may result in disciplinary action, including termination and legal proceedings.
