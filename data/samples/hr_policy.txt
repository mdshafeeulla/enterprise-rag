# HR - Employee Leave Policy

## Overview
All full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per calendar year.

## Types of Leave
1. **Annual Leave**: Accrued at a rate of 1.67 days per month.
2. **Sick Leave**: 10 days per year for illness or injury. Requires a doctor's note for more than 3 consecutive days.
3. **Maternity Leave**: 26 weeks of paid leave for new mothers.
4. **Paternity Leave**: 2 weeks of paid leave for new fathers.

## Requesting Leave
Leave must be requested via the HR portal at least 2 weeks in advance for annual leave. Sick leave should be reported by 9:00 AM on the first day of absence.

## Carry-over
Up to 5 days of unused annual leave can be carried over to the next calendar year. These must be used by March 31st.
